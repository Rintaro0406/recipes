# Guacamole
## Ingredients
* avocado
* lemon
* salt
* extra virgin olive oil from kalamata
* garlic
* chili pepper from calabria
* pasta (RUMMO is recommended)
* petersilien

## Instructions
* peel the avocado
* remove avocado, wasting is bad, give this avocado to whoever likes avocado so much
* drink lemon juice, it is good to wake up
* clean the bowl
* put extra-virgin oil in the pan
* sauté garlic and pepper
* put pasta water and add petersilien
* mix with pasta, pasta should be al dente
* now guacamole is finished, vegan, environmentally friendly recipe
